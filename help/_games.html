Playing a Game

To move a piece, you click on it, and then click on the square that you want to move it to. If the move is legal, it will be played. Otherwise, it won't.

To have the engine play a move, either click on the play icon in the window's titlebar or go to Game>Engine Move. The engine will play a move, at the playing power it is set to (more on engine power here.)

To have Schach play a random move, either click on the loop icon in the window's titlebar or go to Game>Random Move. A random move will be played.
