Status Frames

Schach has status frames to either side of the board. They display information like current play status (check, etc.), game history, and remaining pieces. To go to a move in the move stack, simply double-click on it, and the game will automatically be undone up to the move (not including the move). All the moves can be redone, as well: go to Edit>Redo All. More on undo and redo here.

To show/hide the status frames, go to View>Toggle Status Frames.
